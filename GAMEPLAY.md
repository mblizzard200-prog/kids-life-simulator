# Gameplay Guide

## Objective
Earn brownie points by finishing all 5 tasks while preventing mom's anger from reaching 100%.

## Task Flow
1. Go to **Sink** and press **E**.
2. Go to **Toy** and press **E**.
3. Go to **Stove** and press **E**.
4. Go to **Friend** and press **E**.
5. Go to **PlayArea** and press **E**.

## UI
- **Brownie Points**: top-left
- **Anger Meter + text**: top-center
- **Current Task**: center
- **Interaction Prompt**: bottom-center when in range
- **Game Over**: center, shown at max anger

## Win/Lose
- **Win state**: all tasks complete message appears.
- **Lose state**: mom's anger reaches 100%.
